25) TipDrop- TipDrop is a micro blogging site, similar to twitter.Site gives you the option to keep 75% of the ad revenues that are created at your tip sheets.They have a referral system as well.

26) Infopirate- Once a great social bookmarking site, there where many members of this site, who made hundreds of dollars a month from just making backlinks.The site had extremely high page rank.But that sadly, didnÂ´t last long.In july 2009, the pagerank of infopirate lowered drastically.After that the site went blank.But itÂ´s up and running again, hopefully itÂ´s still work in progress.If you are looking for a easy backlink and want to make some money while doing it, this is a very good site and hopefully will improve in the future.They have a 80/20 ad revenue.You get 80 percent and infopirate gets 20 percent.

31) oDesk - is a place where people looking for freelance work and people looking to hire a freelancer can meet, arrange jobs, send and receive payments, and everything else that is required to work in the freelance realm.
